The Refersion for WooCommerce plugin allows shop owners to connect their WooCommerce shop with a Refersion account.

== Description ==
Imagine generating more sales through commission based partnerships with Bloggers, Internet Marketers, Publishers, and so forth. Using Refersion, you\'ll be able to track performance for all of your Affiliates and generate more streams of revenue at the same time.

It only takes a few minutes to setup your Affiliate Program and there is zero technical knowledge required. Just take a look at our Video Quick Start Guide.

Here are some additional features that you can expect in Refersion:

* Complete end-to-end tracking

We do all of the backend tracking for you. You'll know things like which Affiliate drove the sale, the specific order, and their total commission.

* We support PayPal!

Pay commissions to Affiliates easily with our PayPal Mass Payments integration.

* Recruit Affiliates

Use your customized Refersion Affiliate Registration page, also called your Business Profile page, to sign up new Affiliates into your program. An example is shown in the screenshots on the right.

* Swift support

If you're stuck we're here to help and listen. Just click on the little badge on the right of your screen within Refersion to contact us or to submit a feature request. We'll get back to you within 24-hours.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `refersion-for-woocommerce` directory into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the Settings > Refersion section in your admin panel to add your Refersion API keys and to enable the plugin.
